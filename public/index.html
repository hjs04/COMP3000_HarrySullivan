About ShipShape Systems

ShipShape Systems is a management application primarily aimed at shipyards that provides the ability to observe and control various different aspects of a shipyard.

Key Features

  • Inventory Management: A simple but effective stock management system.
  • Finances: Add items from the inventory to invoices which can then be printed.
  • Employee Management: Create, edit and delete users, and assign their access privileges, but only when logged in as an administrator.
  • Ship Hub: Track incoming and outgoing shipments. COMING SOON.

Why Choose ShipShape Systems?

This application is highly effective for shipyard management while also being simple enough for anyone to use - regardless of their technical expertise.
